Dumbbell Pull Over

The dumbbell pullover is a weightlifting exercise that targets your chest and lat muscles. Perform the dumbbell pullover exercise by grabbing a single dumbbell and lying back on a flat bench.

How it Works:

    1]Hold a medium-to-heavy dumbbell according to your strength, lay with your back flat on a bench or stability ball.
    2]your feet planted on the ground and your core engaged, extend your arms to the sky, cupping the dumbbell with Withboth hands above your chest.
    3]You should be holding the far end of the dumbbell in both palms, with your fingers and thumbs against the handle, and your arms extended.
    4].Keeping your low back pressed into the bench or stability ball, slowly lower your arms overhead until your biceps reach your ears.
    5] Slowly bring your arms back to above your chest and repeat
