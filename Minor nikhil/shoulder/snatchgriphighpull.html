Snatch Grip High pull-down

The snatch grip high pull is a variation of the high pull and an exercise used primarily to target the muscles of the shoulders and traps. In given picture he uses his legs coming off to the ground trying to push the platform. Down hips and shoulders move up at the same rate. And the lifter keeps the arching his back throughout the whole lift.

How It Works:

    1]Grab the bar 4-6 inches wider then shoulder width apart and stand with your feet about shoulder width apart.
    2]Bend at the knees and squat down like you are sitting in a chair.
    3]Keep the bar in tight to your body.Keep the bar in tight to your body,
    4]As you pull the bar above your knees bounce the bar slightly off your quads.
    5]Now pull the bar up to your sternum and thrust your hips forward as you do.
    6]Bring the bar back to your waist and then squat down and set the weight back on the floor.
